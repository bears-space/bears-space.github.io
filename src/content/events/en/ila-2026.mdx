ILA or **International Aerospace Exhibition** (or *Internationale Luft- und Raumfahrtausstellung*) 2026 was held in Berlin from 10 June to 14 June where BEARS team participated with it's exciting Projects that it has been working on so far. In this Event, There were many International and National Companies which presented exciting Opportunities to Grow, Learn and Rebuild.&#x20;
