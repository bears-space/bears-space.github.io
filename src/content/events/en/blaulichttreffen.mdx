**Blaulichttreffen 2026** is a large public event at Flugplatz Bienenfarm featuring more than 150 emergency vehicles, live demonstrations by fire and rescue services, helicopters, career information stands, family activities, and scenic flights with historic aircraft. The event takes place on **9 May 2026** and is designed as a family-friendly day centered around emergency services and aviation. This year there is a new addition: the topic of “spaceflight,” where BEARS will exhibit its displays. You can find out more on the [Blaulichttreffen website](https://www.flugplatz-bienenfarm.de/veranstaltungen/blaulichttreffen-2026/) and in the [spaceflight participant list](https://www.flugplatz-bienenfarm.de/veranstaltungen/blaulichttreffen-2026/blaulichttreffen-2026-teilnehemerliste-raumfahrt/)!
